Privacy Policy

Intro

Hello! Welcome to the 2048 GENESIS Privacy Policy. This policy describes how Hadil ABAILE collects, uses, processes, and shares information about you when you download, access, and use the 2048 GENESIS mobile game.

This Privacy Policy applies solely to your download, access, and/or use of the "2048 GENESIS" mobile application (the "Game"). It also applies to any information collected or processed through the Game's associated online features, such as the leaderboard powered by Firebase and the account management system via Google Sign-In, as well as the data deletion request process facilitated through our designated webpage.

When you download, access, or play our Game, you acknowledge the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy, including the use of data by third-party services integrated into the Game (such as Google Firebase and Google AdMob) for purposes like analytics, account management, and advertising, as described in the relevant sections of this Privacy Policy. If you have any concerns about providing information to us, or it being used as described in this Privacy Policy, you should not use our Game.

What Data We Collect

When you use the 2048 GENESIS game, we collect certain information about you and your device. This data is collected either directly by the Game or through integrated third-party SDKs and services (like Google Sign-In, Google Firebase, and Google AdMob).

The types of data we collect include:

Personal Information:

  • Name: The username you provide or that is associated with your Google Sign-In, used for your profile and leaderboards.
  • Email Address: Collected via Google Sign-In for authentication and account management purposes.
  • User IDs: Unique identifiers associated with your game account for authentication and linking your data.
  • Other Information: Your Country, as provided or associated with your profile.

Location:

  • Approximate location: General location information (e.g., city or region), which may be collected by integrated SDKs for analytics and advertising purposes. We do not collect precise GPS location.

App Activity:

  • App interactions: Information about how you interact with our Game, such as the screens you view and buttons you tap.
  • Other user-generated content: Data that you generate through your use of the Game, primarily your High Score.
  • Other actions: Data about specific gameplay events or milestones tracked for analytical purposes.

Device or other IDs:

  • Device or other IDs: Identifiers such as your Android Advertising ID, used by integrated advertising and analytics services to recognize your device.

We do not collect any data related to Financial Information, Health and Fitness, Messages, Photos and Videos, Audio Files, Files and Documents, Calendar, Contacts, or Web Browse history.

How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect about you for the following purposes:

To Provide and Maintain App Functionality:

We use your data (including Username, Email Address, User IDs, High Score, and Country if displayed) to operate and provide the core features of the 2048 GENESIS game, such as enabling you to log in with Google Sign-In, accessing and displaying the leaderboard, and saving your game progress and High Score. Integrated third-party SDKs (like Firebase) also use data (including Device or other IDs) to support the app's underlying functions.

For Analytics and Performance:

We use your data (including User IDs, Approximate location, App interactions, Other user-generated content - High Score, Other actions - Gameplay events, and Device or other IDs) to understand how users interact with our Game, monitor its performance, identify popular features, track gameplay patterns, and analyze usage trends (e.g., understanding where our players are located geographically). This helps us to improve the game and your experience.

For Advertising and Marketing:

We use certain data (including Approximate location and Device or other IDs) to display advertisements within the Game and to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. This data is processed by integrated advertising SDKs (like Google AdMob) to provide personalized ads, where permitted.

For Account Management:

We use your data (including Username, Email Address, User IDs, High Score, and Country) to set up, manage, and authenticate your game account linked via Google Sign-In, and to store and associate your game progress and data with your account in our Firebase database.

For Fraud Prevention, Security, and Compliance:

We may use certain data (including Username, Email Address, User IDs, High Score, and Device or other IDs) as part of our efforts to prevent fraud, ensure the security of the Game and user accounts, and comply with legal obligations. This includes ensuring the integrity of the leaderboard and protecting against unauthorized access.

We only use your data for the purposes for which it was collected, and as described in this Privacy Policy.

How We Share Your Data

We share certain collected data with third parties that are integrated into our Game to provide specific services and functionality. Data is shared only for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

The main categories of third parties with whom data is shared include:

Google Firebase:

We use Google Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Analytics. Data (such as Username, Email Address, User IDs, Country, High Score, App interactions, Other actions, and Device or other IDs) is shared with Firebase to enable Google Sign-In authentication, manage user accounts, store and display leaderboard data, and for analytics purposes to understand game usage and performance.

Google AdMob:

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the Game. Certain data (such as Approximate location and Device or other IDs) is shared with AdMob and its advertising partners to serve relevant ads and measure ad effectiveness.

Google Play Services:

Your data (including User IDs and Email Address) may be shared with Google Play Services as part of the Google Sign-In and authentication process.

These third parties operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies to understand their data handling practices. You can find more information about Google's data practices on their privacy policy page: https://policies.google.com/privacy (Note: This is a link to Google's general Privacy Policy)

We do not sell your personal data.

Data Retention

We retain collected data for different periods depending on the type of data and the purpose for which it was collected.

User Account Data:

We retain the personal information and game data associated with your account (such as Username, Email Address, User IDs, Country, and High Score) for as long as your game account exists in our Firebase database. This data is necessary to provide you with access to the Game, maintain your profile, and display your progress on the leaderboard.

Analytics Data:

Data collected for analytics purposes (such as Approximate location, App interactions, Other actions, and Device or other IDs) is retained by Firebase Analytics for a period necessary for analysis and reporting, as configured in our Firebase project settings. This data is typically retained for a limited period and may be aggregated or anonymized over time.

Third-Party Services Data:

Data collected and processed by integrated third-party services like Google AdMob is retained according to the data retention policies of those respective services.

When your user account is deleted upon request, the user account data directly stored in our Firebase database (Username, Email Address, Country, High Score, and associated User IDs) is permanently removed. We also delete associated identifiers from our records. Retention periods for analytics or advertising data held by third parties are governed by their policies.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting the security of your data. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect the data we collect and store.

Key security measures include:

Encryption in Transit:

Data transmitted between your device and our services, as well as between our services and integrated third parties (like Firebase and AdMob), is encrypted using secure protocols (such as HTTPS). This helps protect your data from unauthorized access during transmission over the internet.

Secure Storage:

We store your data on secure servers managed by Google Firebase, a reputable third-party provider that implements robust security measures to protect the data they store. Access to this data is restricted.

Reliance on Third-Party Security:

We rely on the security measures and practices implemented by the third-party services we integrate into our Game (such as Google Firebase and Google AdMob) to protect the data they collect and process.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. We continuously review and update our security practices as necessary.
